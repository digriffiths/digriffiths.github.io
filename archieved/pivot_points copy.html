Pivot Point Trading Strategy

Overview

This pivot point trading strategy is inspired by the legendary trader Jesse Livermore. Pivot points are key levels on a chart where price tends to pivot or reverse, making them important areas to watch for potential trading opportunities. This strategy involves identifying these pivot points and using them as entry and exit points for trades. To manage risk, it includes a stop loss, which is a predetermined point at which the trade will be exited if the market moves in the opposite direction.

    Methodology


    Import btc data

  • First, we import bitcoin daily data. I chose 2017 - 2023 so we have a large time period to experiment with.

    • Find & Plot Pivot Points

  • The logic behind determining a pivot points involves tracking the max close price within a certain time period e.g. 10 days and setting an amount of time it must be the max value e.g. 5 days. For example, if i track at a 10 day window and the price hits a a high of $10, price must stay below $10 for 5 days for this price point be considered a pivot point. Theese variables can be changed easily. See plot below showing pivot points

    • Build Strategy

  • With the pivot points identified, the next step is to formulate a trading strategy based on the findings. To execute this strategy, we look to enter a long position when the price breaks through a pivot point above, and a short position when the price breaks through a pivot point below. To confirm a breakout, a buffer of 5% is applied, requiring the price to surpass the pivot point by this amount before we take a position. To mitigate risk, a stop loss is implemented to switch our position to neutral if the price moves in the opposite direction.
